Employment Opportunities

About SpanLula

SpanLula is an initiative of KoenaConcepts, a Pan-African technology company developing innovative technology solutions to socio-economic challenges.

SpanLula is "Bridging the Digital Skills Gap in Africa" by enabling young people in the rural areas & townships to conveniently access technology skills training & job opportunities. The initiative is also aimed at assisting Recruiters in Public and Private Organizations with efficiently recruiting for the hard-to-fill vacancies that require ICT-skilled candidates.
